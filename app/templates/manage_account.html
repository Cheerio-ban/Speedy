>Close Account

Before an account is closed, please, make sure to transfer all funds present in the account to another account. Failure to do so can result in loss of funds and an email will have to be written to the service provider to trace and recover the funds. Also, for future ease, printing some part of the transactions history is advised, as transactions from closed accounts cannot be recovered unless appropriate authorities request it.

>Change your account pin

Account pin can only be changed a month after the previous pin was changed. It is advised to store ypur account pin in a safe place, because, account pin recovery process can only be initiated by the service providers after an email is sent. This is to reduce any chance of fraudulent activities.
